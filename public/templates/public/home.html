Snowdesk

Avalanche bulletins for backcountry skiers.

We take the daily SLF avalanche forecast and present it in a clear, mobile-friendly format so you can make better decisions in the mountains.

Daily bulletins — fetched automatically from the Swiss Institute for Snow and Avalanche Research (SLF).

Per-region detail — danger level, avalanche problems, snowpack structure, and field guidance for every forecast region.

Season archives — browse the full season history for any region.
